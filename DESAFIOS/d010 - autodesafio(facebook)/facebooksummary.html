What is Facebook?

Facebook is a social media website that allows friends, families, colleagues, etc., to connect with one another, sharing multiple text and media content, including pictures, videos, articles, music, etc.

Who created Facebook?

Facebook was created by Mark Zuckerberg, Andrew McCollum, Eduardo Saverin, Chris Hughes, and Dustin Moskovitz.

When was Facebook invented?

2003.

What is a brief history of Facebook?

Facebook was originally created in 2004 as a student directory for Harvard students. However, the company expanded with time, making the social directory available worldwide. Currently, there are 2.89billion active users of Facebook.

What was Facebook's old name?

Facemash

Who was the first Facebook user?

Mark Zuckerberg

Whose face was first uploaded to Facebook?

Al Pacino

What is Facebook known for?

Facebook is known as an easy-to-use social media platform. Other business owners also use Facebook as an advertising medium to connect with their potential customers.
